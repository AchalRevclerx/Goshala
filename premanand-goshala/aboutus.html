Welcome to श्री प्रेमानंद गोशाला

श्री प्रेमानंद गोशाला is a sacred cow shelter (गोशाला) dedicated to the protection, care, and welfare of cows. Established with the divine vision of serving गौ माता (Mother Cow), our goshala is a haven for abandoned, old, injured, and stray cows who find love, compassion, and a dignified life here.

We believe that गौ सेवा ही मानव सेवा — serving cows is serving humanity. Our goshala is not just a shelter; it is a spiritual sanctuary where cows are revered as mothers and cared for with utmost devotion. Rooted in the timeless principles of Sanatana Dharma, we strive to uphold the sacred tradition of cow protection (गौ संरक्षण) that has been an integral part of Indian culture for millennia.

At Premanand Goshala, every cow receives nutritious feed, clean water, regular veterinary check-ups, and a loving environment. We are committed to giving these gentle beings the respect and dignity they deserve.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, loving, and nurturing home for abandoned, old, injured, and stray cows. To promote गौ संरक्षण (cow protection) and गौ सेवा (cow service) as a way of life, and to spread awareness about the spiritual, cultural, and ecological significance of cows in Sanatana Dharma.

Our Vision

To create a world where all cows are treated with love, dignity, and respect. We envision a society where every cow is protected, every गौशाला is self-sustaining, and the sacred bond between humans and cows is restored as per the eternal values of Sanatana Dharma.

Our Values

Compassion, devotion, selfless service (सेवा), environmental sustainability, and reverence for all life. We are guided by the principle that protecting a cow is equivalent to protecting all of creation, and serving गौ माता is the highest form of worship.

Our Focus Areas

  • Cow Protection (गौ संरक्षण): Rescuing and sheltering abandoned, injured, and old cows. Providing them with lifelong care, medical treatment, and a safe environment.
  • Go-Seva (गौ सेवा): Encouraging devotees and volunteers to participate in daily cow service activities such as feeding, bathing, and grooming the cows.
  • Organic Farming: Utilizing cow dung and cow urine (गौ मूत्र) for preparing organic fertilizers, pesticides, and compost. Promoting chemical-free, sustainable agriculture.
  • Cow-Based Education: Conducting workshops, seminars, and educational programs on the importance of cows in Sanatana Dharma, Vedic culture, and sustainable living.
  • Cow Adoption Program: Allowing devotees and well-wishers to sponsor or adopt a cow (गौ गोदान) and contribute to its maintenance and well-being.
  • Panchagavya Products: Producing and promoting Panchagavya (five products from cow) based products including medicines, soaps, and fertilizers for holistic health and farming.
  • Community Outreach: Organizing गौ पूजा, गौ दान समारोह, and community feasts to spread the message of cow protection and involve the community in our mission.
  • Gaushala Sustainability: Developing self-sustaining models for gaushalas through go-based products, biogas, and organic farming initiatives.

Our Strategies

  • 建立完善的救援网络，与各地志愿者合作，及时救助无家可归和受伤的牛只。
  • 提供全面的兽医护理，包括定期体检、疫苗接种、疾病治疗和特殊护理。
  • 开发和推广基于牛粪和牛尿的有机肥料，支持有机农业和环保事业。
  • 通过社交媒体、网站和社区活动传播牛保护的重要性，提高公众意识。
  • 与寺庙、学校和社会组织合作，开展牛保护教育和文化活动。
  • 建立透明的捐赠和赞助体系，确保每一分钱都用于牛的福祉。
  • 持续改善基础设施，为牛只提供更舒适、更卫生的生活环境。

Our Achievements

  • Successfully rescued and sheltered over 200+ cows including abandoned, injured, and old cows.
  • Conducted 50+ medical camps providing free veterinary check-ups and treatment for cows.
  • Distributed over 10,000 kg of nutritious fodder and feed to cows in need.
  • Organized 25+ गौ पूजा and गौ दान ceremonies with active community participation.
  • Established an organic farming unit producing cow dung-based fertilizers and pesticides.
  • Educated 5000+ people through workshops and awareness programs on cow protection.
  • Built new cow sheds with modern amenities to house 100+ additional cows.

Our Journey

2020 - The Beginning

श्री प्रेमानंद गोशाला was founded with a small group of 5 cows and a vision to create a safe haven for abandoned cows. The initial shelter was a modest setup on a small piece of land.

2021 - First Expansion

With the blessings of saints and support from the community, the goshala expanded to accommodate 30 cows. Regular medical check-ups and a proper feeding schedule were established.

2022 - Community Engagement

Organized the first गौ पूजा महोत्सव which saw participation from over 1000 devotees. Launched the cow adoption (गौ गोदान) program to involve the community in cow protection.

2023 - Organic Initiative

Started the organic farming wing, producing cow dung-based fertilizers and promoting chemical-free agriculture. Reached a milestone of 100 rescued cows.

2024 - Infrastructure Growth

New state-of-the-art cow sheds were constructed with proper ventilation, water supply, and medical facilities. Launched awareness campaigns and educational workshops.

2025-2026 - Ongoing Mission

Today, श्री प्रेमानंद गोशाला is home to over 200 cows. We continue to expand our reach, rescue more cows, and spread the message of गौ संरक्षण across the nation.
