About Us

Our Mission

To rescue, shelter, and care for abandoned and stray cows while promoting organic farming and sustainable living. We aim to create a world where every cow is treated with love and respect, and where traditional farming practices are preserved for future generations.

Welcome to Our Gaushala

Welcome to Shri Premand Gaushala, a sacred place dedicated to the protection and care of cows. Founded with a deep love for these gentle creatures, our Gaushala has been serving the community and preserving Indian traditions for years. We believe that cows are not just animals but are considered sacred in our culture, representing Mother Earth's abundance and nourishment.

Our History
Early Years

Shri Premand Gaushala was established with a small group of cows and a big dream. What started as a simple shelter has grown into a comprehensive cow protection center serving hundreds of animals daily.

Expansion & Growth

Over the years, we expanded our facilities to include modern veterinary care, organic farming demonstrations, educational programs, and community outreach initiatives. Our herd grew from 5 to over 100 cows.

Today & Beyond

Today, we stand as a model Gaushala with state-of-the-art facilities, an organic dairy, a goshala museum, and a vibrant community of volunteers and supporters dedicated to cow welfare.
